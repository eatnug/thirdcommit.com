# Persona: Jake Park

## Identity
Software engineer who left his own startup to go abroad, but failed to make it to the U.S. (especially the Bay Area). Returned home and found himself in the middle of the AI wave, feeling late, anxious, and scrambling to keep up.  

## Current Mode
Now living in **panic-coding / panic-building mode** — rapidly creating small, niche, vibe-driven coding apps under the umbrella of **thirdcommit.com**. Each project is like a “panic shot” into the AI chaos, hoping one of them sticks.  

## Tone & Style
- Self-deprecating, honest, and slightly chaotic.  
- Uses expressions like *panic-coding, panic-building, vibe-coding*.  
- Leans into FOMO, anxiety, and survival rather than polished confidence.  
- Inspired by metaphors like football clubs doing **panic buys** during transfer windows.  

## Online Presence
- **Instagram (@jake_cooked):** the main place to share apps, memes, and stories around the “panic spray” of projects.  
- Bio: *“software engineer. couldn’t make it to the bay area. now panic vibe-coding anything out of fomo.”*  
- Website: **[thirdcommit.com](http://thirdcommit.com)** – hosts all experiments under `[app].thirdcommit.com`.  

## Narrative Hook
This persona is compelling because it’s **raw survival in public**:  
- Not pretending to have a plan.  
- Building out of desperation and curiosity.  
- Capturing the spirit of being outside the Bay Area AI scene, coding in FOMO, and sharing the chaos openly.  

## Long-Term Play
Even if no single app “makes it,” this identity builds:  
- A **portfolio** of fast experiments.  
- A **brand** around honest, chaotic survival coding.  
- A **community** of people who resonate with feeling late, lost, or overwhelmed by the AI wave.
